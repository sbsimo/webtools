Leaf density in plant canopies - Oct-Nov 2023

This dashboard shows the average values and anomalies of leaf density in plant canopies, extracted on the CLMS Leaf Area Index (LAI). The results displayed here refer to the period indicated above.

Anomalies are calculated with reference to the long term average for the same yearly period; the time series starts in 2014.
